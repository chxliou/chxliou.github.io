## Chenxi Liu (he/him)

I am currently a Ph.D student at [TMLR Group @ Hong Kong](https://github.com/tmlr-group), supervised by [Prof. Bo Han](https://bhanml.github.io/). I work as a visiting student at [MBZUAI](https://mbzuai.ac.ae/research/department/machine-learning-department/), collaborating with [Prof. Kun Zhang](https://www.andrew.cmu.edu/user/kunz1/) and [Dr. Yongqiang Chen](https://lfhase.win/).
